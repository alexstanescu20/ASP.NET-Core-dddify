# Sponsors

Dddify is an open-source project licensed under MIT, and its documentation remains freely available. Sponsorship is completely voluntary and does not affect any developer's access, usage, or contribution. 

If Dddify has helped with your learning, project practice, or team development, sponsorship is welcome to support ongoing maintenance.

## Use of Sponsorship

Sponsorship funds are mainly used for:

- Core code development and long-term maintenance.
- Bug fixes, compatibility adaptation, and version releases.
- Documentation, tutorials, and best-practice materials.
- Validation of production-grade practices and accumulation of implementation experience.
- Infrastructure costs such as servers and domains.

## Individual Sponsorship

Individual users can support the project through WeChat sponsorship. Sponsoring any amount from ¥99 to ¥199 grants permanent access to the Dddify member service WeChat group, where you can get answers related to framework usage, practical implementation, and troubleshooting.

## Corporate Sponsorship

Companies and organizations can support the continued development of Dddify through sponsorship. Corporate sponsors can receive priority support for feedback, practical requirements, and improvement suggestions, helping the project better fit real business scenarios.

Sponsor benefits:

- Sponsor information displayed on the official website homepage.
- Sponsor information displayed on documentation content pages.

Sponsor display content should be consistent with open-source communities, developer tools, technical services, or related professional fields. The specific display format is subject to mutual confirmation.
